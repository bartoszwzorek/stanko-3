About

Learn More About Me

As a person who values individual charm of every woman, I do not like exaggerated make-up or pigmentation.

I have been interested in body decoration for many years. Before discovering the passion for permanent make-up, I had explored the secrets of tattoo. Today I appreciate the knowledge which I managed to acquire. In the profession of a lingerist it is very important to know the difference between permanent make-up and tattoo.

It is the subtle pigmentation and natural effects which have been the greatest inspiration right from the start and encouraged me to pursue the profession of a linergist. Make-up as such has never stolen my heart. Of course, I put on my make-up every day and I follow the cosmetic branch with great attention and interest but, at the same time, I am of the opinion that heavy make-up does not highlight our beauty.

As a person who values individual charm of every woman, I do not like exaggerated make-up or pigmentation. Each of us has great reserves of beauty. It is sometimes enough to emphasise our good looks in a skilful and subtle way to bring out their natural glamour.


At work I am very demanding and diligent. I know that hard and systematic work as well as the willingness to develop oneself are the key to success. Aiming at perfection, caring about the smallest details and building good relationships with clients, combined with true love and passion for the job, are the factors necessary to be professional in PMU business. I will share my achievements with you with pleasure.

Permanent make-up

Permanent make-up is an alternative to traditional make-up. In the case of eyebrows, the procedure is aimed at shaping them, getting rid of irregularities of superciliary arches, correcting eyebrow asymmetry – if it is not too large.

Permanent make-up is to emphasise the beauty of clients so that it is in harmony with the natural features of their faces, and to create a lasting beautifying effect.
