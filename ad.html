F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is the procedure painful?

    Pain perception and resistance to pain are individual matters. As I care about our mutual comfort, I use lidocaine-based anaesthesia during the procedure.After anaesthesia, pigmentation is almost not perceptible. It has happened a few times that the client even fell asleep during the procedure.

  • How long does the procedure take?

    The procedure of applying permanent make-up should last approximately 3 hours. Pigmentation is preceded with a consultation so I make individual time arrangements depending on the needs of each client. More...

  • How long does permanent-makeup last?

    The effects of permanent make-up last from 6 to 18 months. It is, however, possible that the pigment or its remains will stay in the skin much longer – even for up to 4 years. More...

  • Is the procedure safe?

    Service quality and safety of my clients are very important to me, which is why I perform the procedure with the professional Quantum device, designed for aesthetic pigmentation. I pay great attention to hygiene at work and observe appropriate washing and disinfection procedures. I use disposable needles, which I open right before the procedure in the presence of my client. More...
